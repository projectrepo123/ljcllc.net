Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Who We Are

LJC Construction is a full-service contractor based in Crystal City, MO, serving Jefferson County and the surrounding area. This policy explains how we handle information you submit through this website.

Information We Collect

When you submit the estimate request form on this site, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, project address, and any project details you choose to share. We do not collect any information automatically beyond what your browser sends to any website (such as your general location or browser type).

How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to respond to your estimate request and communicate with you about your project. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How Your Information Is Stored

Form submissions are processed by Formspree and delivered to our email inbox. This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies if you have questions about infrastructure-level data handling.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as needed to complete or follow up on your project inquiry. We do not maintain a marketing database or CRM system.

Your Rights

You may request that we delete any personal information you have submitted to us by contacting us at home@ljcllc.net or by calling (314) 649-3111.
