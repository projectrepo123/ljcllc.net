Siding in Jefferson County

Tight Installs, No Gaps, Done Right.

Siding is the primary defense against moisture, wind, and heat loss. A bad install leads to rot, mold, and insulation problems that cost far more to fix later. LJC Construction installs vinyl, fiber cement (Hardie and equivalent), and wood siding on residential and light commercial properties throughout Jefferson County.

We also handle fascia and soffit replacement, often bundled with a roofing or gutter job. If you have damaged sections from impact or moisture intrusion, Joe comes out, looks at the whole picture, and tells you what needs to happen.

What's Included

  • Full moisture barrier / house wrap installation
  • Vinyl or fiber cement siding panels
  • J-channel and trim finishing
  • Fascia and soffit replacement
  • Corner post and starter strip installation
  • Window and door trim wrap
  • Old siding removal and disposal
  • Caulking and sealing at all penetrations
  • Workmanship warranty on all installs
